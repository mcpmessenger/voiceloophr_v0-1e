1. Introduction

Welcome to VoiceLoop HR ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered document analysis platform and related services (collectively, the "Service").

VoiceLoop HR is a comprehensive document processing platform that combines AI-powered text extraction, semantic search, voice interaction, and intelligent document processing to help organizations manage their HR documents and content.

Important: By using our Service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account or authenticate through third-party services (Google, LinkedIn, Microsoft), we collect your name, email address, and profile information.
  • Documents: Files you upload including PDFs, Word documents, Excel files, PowerPoint presentations, text files, images, audio, and video files.
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings when you use our speech-to-text and voice chat features.
  • API Keys: Optional API keys you provide for enhanced functionality (OpenAI, ElevenLabs).
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, including pages visited, features used, and time spent.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and IP address.
  • Analytics Data: Performance metrics and error logs to improve our Service.
  • Session Data: Temporary data stored in browser localStorage for functionality.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Services: Profile information, calendar events, and Drive documents (with your permission).
  • LinkedIn: Professional profile data and network information.
  • Microsoft: Calendar data and authentication information.
  • Social Media: Content from integrated platforms like Facebook and Twitter.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Process and analyze your documents using AI technologies
  • Provide semantic search and document retrieval capabilities
  • Enable voice interaction and transcription services
  • Generate document summaries and insights
  • Facilitate calendar integration and scheduling

3.2 Authentication and Security

  • Authenticate users and manage account access
  • Implement security measures and fraud prevention
  • Monitor for unauthorized access or misuse

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our Service
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Optimize performance and user experience
  • Conduct research and analytics

3.4 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Locations

Your data may be stored in the following locations:

  • Browser Storage: Temporary data in localStorage for guest users
  • Server Memory: Temporary processing data during active sessions
  • Supabase Database: Persistent storage for authenticated users
  • AWS Services: Document processing and storage infrastructure
  • Third-Party APIs: Processing through OpenAI, ElevenLabs, and other services

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and authorization protocols
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and user permission management
  • Monitoring and logging of system activities
Development Notice: Currently in development mode, some documents may be temporarily stored in shared server memory. This will be replaced with proper user-based access controls in production.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with several third-party services to provide our functionality:

5.1 AI and Processing Services

  • OpenAI: GPT-4 for document analysis, Whisper for speech transcription
  • ElevenLabs: Text-to-speech synthesis for voice responses
  • AWS Textract: Advanced document text extraction and OCR

5.2 Authentication and Storage

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and user management
  • Google OAuth: Authentication and Drive/Calendar integration
  • LinkedIn OAuth: Professional network authentication
  • Microsoft OAuth: Calendar and Office integration

5.3 Analytics and Monitoring

  • Vercel Analytics: Usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Browser APIs: Local storage and session management

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they handle your data.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality and security
  • Authentication Cookies: Maintain your login session
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns (Vercel Analytics)

6.2 Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Authentication tokens
  • Uploaded files (in guest mode)
  • API keys (when provided by user)

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Service.

7. Data Retention

7.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Documents: Retained until deleted by user or account closure
  • Processing Data: Temporary data deleted after processing completion
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data retained for up to 2 years
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings processed and immediately deleted

7.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. We will:

  • Remove your documents and associated data
  • Delete your account and profile information
  • Remove data from third-party services where possible
  • Retain minimal data required for legal compliance

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

8.2 EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EU or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to restrict processing and data portability.

8.3 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are in California, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale.

8.4 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information provided in the Contact section. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
